Our Story

Curating beauty and elegance for every home

The Beginning

My Story as a Shopkeeper

Hi, I'm Anand Singh. My journey into interior styling began as a personal passion for creating spaces that feel both luxurious and welcoming. Over the years, I noticed how a single, well-placed artifact could completely transform the dynamic of a room.

That realization led to the birth of Galaxy Furniture and Interior. What started as a small curated collection has grown into a beloved destination for homeowners looking to tell their own unique stories through design.

Our Values

Sourcing Philosophy

We believe in conscious curation. Every piece at Galaxy Furniture is handpicked with intention.

From handcrafted local pottery to ethically imported vintage mirrors, our sourcing focuses on sustainability, authentic craftsmanship, and timeless design. We partner directly with artisans and responsible workshops to ensure that every product you bring into your home has a story you can feel good about.
